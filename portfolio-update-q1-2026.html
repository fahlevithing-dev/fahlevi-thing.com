Portfolio Update Q1 2026

Here is my portfolio update for the first quarter of 2026. The US-Israel/Iran conflict set the tone for the entire quarter: the IHSG dropped sharply at the end of January, foreign investors turned into persistent sellers, and the pressure never really lifted through February and March. The table below shows all my positions as of the end of the quarter.

For comparison, here is the same portfolio at the end of January, before the selloff gathered speed. The two tables tell the story in one number: total unrealized gain fell from 28 percent to 18 percent in two months. The nickel position in DKFT held on to most of its triple-digit gain, while property and banking names like PWON and BBCA took the deepest cuts. The plan itself did not change: no panic selling, keep collecting dividends, and let cheap prices become an opportunity rather than a reason to leave.

Sebagai perbandingan, ini adalah portofolio yang sama pada akhir Januari, sebelum aksi jual semakin kencang.
